Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

What We Do

Calendar Booking ("the Service") allows business owners to share their calendar availability and lets their customers book appointments directly.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Business owners: Google account email address and calendar availability data (free/busy times only) via Google Calendar API, accessed through Nylas.
  • Customers: Name and email address provided when booking an appointment.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display available time slots to customers
  • Create calendar events for confirmed bookings
  • Send calendar invitations to participants

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the booking functionality.

Google Calendar Data

We access Google Calendar data solely to:

  • Read free/busy information to determine available time slots
  • Create events on the owner's calendar when a booking is confirmed

We do not read, store, or share the contents of your calendar events. We only access free/busy status. Calendar access credentials are encrypted at rest.

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in encrypted databases
  • Google Calendar access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES encryption
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Nylas — to securely connect to Google Calendar (Nylas Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — for hosting (Vercel Privacy Policy)
  • Neon — for database hosting (Neon Privacy Policy)

Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will remove your calendar connection and all associated booking data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Google Calendar access at any time via your Google Account permissions
